About Me

Hello! My name is Chad Leino, and I am a Senior at the University of Alabama studying Computer Engineering. You may be wondering, why is a Computer Engineering student making their own website? Well it's because I love to make things. Every time I see something interesting I say to myself "I wonder how they made that". And it turns out, websites can be pretty simple, so here we are.

Feel free to look around my website. It's pretty basic as I am still in the begenning of my web development jouney. I have learned all of this from the Odin Project. I heard about the Odin Project from my friend Naeem. He graduated from the University of Auburn a few years ago with a Computer Science degree. But even after graduating, he had a hard time getting the kinds of positions he wanted. In his free time, he completed Odin, which is a full stack web development course. It's completely free and open source. I hope to complete the Odin Project by the time I graduate. But between my classes it can be hard to find the time.
